Marigold Uses Medicinal Use: One of the health benefits of marigolds is that it is used in the treatment of minor burns when used in the form of ointments or a tincture Health benefits and therapeutic uses of marigold flowers as well as the leaves of the marigold include it being beneficial in minimizing the number of tumors in the case of breast cancer as well as in the prevention of the development of new cancer cells One of the medicinal uses of marigolds is that it promotes the growth of both new blood vessels as well as new skin tissue and hence it is used in the healing of wounds such as burns, scrapes as well as irritated skin Culinary Use: The bright petals of signet marigolds add color and a spicy tang to salads and other summer dishes
