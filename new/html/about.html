About us

A nursery is a place where plants are propagated and grown to a desired age. Mostly the plants concerned are for gardening, forestry or conservation biology, rather than agriculture. They include retail nurseries, which sell to the general public, wholesale nurseries, which sell only to businesses such as other nurseries and to commercial gardeners, and private nurseries, which supply the needs of institutions or private estates. Some will also work in plant breeding. Nurseries often grow plants in a greenhouse, a building of glass or in plastic tunnels, designed to protect young plants from harsh weather while allowing access to light and ventilation. Modern greenhouses allow automated control of temperature, ventilation and light and semi-automated watering and feeding. A nursery customer could be a home gardener, landscape gardener or even a commercial farmer. A nursery business must cater a wide range of plants that would be indoor to outdoor plants and container to earth planting plants. Apart from plants, the nurseries must have to have the ability to assist in all kinds of inquiries and purchases.



Our Mission

Our Mission is to make a website -"Kite". Kite was designed keeping in mind user requirements and the problems faced. It is a website for buying not just plants but also seeds, fertilizers and pots online. Information about plants is provided on the website and thus it could also be used for learning more about plants. Users can add product to cart and purchase them. Further a product tracking system is added so that buyers can locate their product.With the increased usage of the internet for day-to-day activities customers prefer buying everything online and saving their time. Moreover online shopping gives variety of options and also helps small businesses grow. We aim to connect buyers to the farmers and help them This project explores the world of farming and having your own nursery .



Our Vision

Website aims to connect user to nearby nursery stores which ensures faster deliveries and good quality products. This not only helps the buyer but also profits small businesses and farmers sell their products.Website also provides information about various plants to save the users time which is otherwise wasted in doing research. It can be accessed all over the internet and the user friendly UI can be easily understood by anyone interested in buying and selling.Since multiple retailers will do business on the site , it will help buyer to compare the prices of same product in one place.
